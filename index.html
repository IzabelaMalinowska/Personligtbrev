My motivation

The dynamic development and diversity of the IT industry contribute to the increase in job offers in this field. There are few sectors, such as working in IT, that guarantee such great development opportunities, while offering high salaries. The IT sector has its own specificity that distinguishes it from other sectors on the labor market. Employees almost never have reasons to complain about the lack of work or unsatisfactory employment conditions. The development of technology drives the search for IT specialists, and completing the Front End Developer course gives me the opportunity to acquire new skills and qualifications in a relatively short time. In addition, the specificity of the IT sector is closely related to constant changes. It is not only creating websites or applications, but also designing and comprehensive implementation of business solutions. Especially in the profession of a programmer, it requires constant mastering of new skills, as well as adaptation to changing conditions and requirements. This gives me great development opportunities, lack of routine and interesting projects. This is why I would like to start this course.
